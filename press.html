The next generation forum software that's free and easy to use.

NodeBB is a next-generation discussion platform that utilizes web sockets for instant interactions and real-time notifications. NodeBB forums have many modern features out of the box such as social network integration and streaming discussions.

  • Founded on May 1, 2014 as NodeBB Inc.

  • There are currently 3 employees.

  • Our team is based out of the Greater Toronto Area, Canada .

  • There are now 31 languages available.

  • 56 contributors and counting.

  • Free and open-sourced code base
